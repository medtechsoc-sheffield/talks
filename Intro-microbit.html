What can you make?

Features

  • 25 red LEDs
  • 2 buttons
  • Motion Sensors: Compass, Accelerometer
  • Light and Temperature
  • Radio and Bluetooth
  • USB and battery connections

  • BBC founded Micro:bit Educational Foundation

  • Plan to give one to every child in UK
